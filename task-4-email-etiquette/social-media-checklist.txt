Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ Do’s

* Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
* Maintain privacy settings and protect personal information
* Use professional language, especially on academic or public platforms
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
* Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions

 ❌ Don’ts

* Do not share offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
* Avoid posting personal details like address, phone number, or passwords
* Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
* Avoid using slang or unprofessional language in formal contexts
* Do not post misleading or fake information
