Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist (for College Students in India)

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Always verify links before clicking—check the URL carefully for spelling errors or fake domains.
2. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or a trusted service.
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps for extra security.
4. Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and avoid using easily guessable details like your birthdate.
5. Beware of urgent messages (e.g., “account blocked” or “offer expires today”)—these are common phishing tactics.
6. UPI Safety Tip: Never approve unknown payment requests (“collect requests”) on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
7. UPI Safety Tip: Do not scan QR codes sent by strangers to “receive money”—this can actually debit your account.
8. Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial transactions such as banking or payments.
9. Install apps only from official stores like Google Play Store and keep them updated.
10. Log out from shared devices and never save passwords on public computers.
